Why we're #1 in Chicago

Clean Ingredients

We do our best to make sure the ingredients we use are the best version of themselves they can be. Our recipes will never have unnecessary filler ingredients so that you can leave with more energy, rather than less.

Locally Sourced

Local farmers are hurting and we aim to support them. Not only is their food more ethical, it's better for you, too!

Fair Prices

We cut out the middleman and pass the savings over to you, so that a healthy meal won't empty your pockets.

Rotating Menu Items

Variety is the spice of life! We make an effort to change our menu items regularly, so the menu will always be just as fresh as the food!

Nightly Specials

From kids and seniors discounts to all-you-can-eat chickpea pasta night, you'll always find something to enjoy at a great price!
